# Data Sources — Topsoil Erosion Monte Carlo Simulation

Channel parameters in `mc_simulation.py` trace to the sources listed here.
The paper (Topsoil Erosion: Pedogenesis Floor) is available on SSRN and contains the full
reference list and §4 calibration narrative.

---

## Channel Sources

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C3_nutrient_replacement`

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C6_subsidy_lock_in`

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*
